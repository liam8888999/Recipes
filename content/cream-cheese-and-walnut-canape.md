## Ingredients

- 300g Australian Cream cheese
- 20g chives, finely chopped
- 5g paprika
- 40g Walnut halves

## Directions

1. Mix the cream cheese and chives thoroughly.
2. Form into balls, 15g each; Dust with paprika.
3. Sandwich each ball between two walnut halves and serve.
