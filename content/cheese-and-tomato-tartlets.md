## Ingredients

- 60g Cream cheese
- 30ml tomato sauce
- 30ml Cream
- paprika
- 10 tartlet cases
- 250ml aspic jelly

## Directions

1. Combine cheese, tomato sauce, cream and paprika in mixer.
2. Pipe into tartlet cases - glaze with aspic.
